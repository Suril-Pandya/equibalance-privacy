Privacy Policy for EquiBalance

Last updated: October 18, 2025

Quick Summary

EquiBalance is a personal finance app that helps you track expenses and manage budgets. We collect minimal data and prioritize your privacy.

Information We Collect

Financial Data

  • Transaction amounts, categories, and descriptions
  • Budget limits and financial goals
  • Expense and income records

Device Information

  • App usage patterns and performance data
  • Device type and operating system
  • Crash reports and error logs

Camera and Microphone

  • Camera: Used only for receipt scanning to extract expense data
  • Microphone: Used only for voice input to add expenses
  • Both are processed locally on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide expense tracking and budget management features
  • Generate financial insights and reports
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All financial data is stored on your device
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted using industry-standard methods
  • No Sharing: We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • No Advertising: We do not use your data for advertising

Your Rights

  • View and export all your data
  • Delete your account and data at any time
  • Control camera and microphone access in app settings
  • Opt out of analytics and crash reporting

Camera and Microphone Usage

Camera Permission

  • Purpose: Scan receipts to automatically extract expense information
  • Processing: Images are processed locally on your device
  • Storage: Images are stored locally and can be deleted anytime
  • Privacy: No images are sent to external servers

Microphone Permission

  • Purpose: Voice input for adding expenses quickly
  • Processing: Audio is converted to text locally on your device
  • Storage: Audio recordings are not permanently stored
  • Privacy: No audio data is sent to external servers

Data Retention

  • Financial data is retained until you delete it
  • App logs are retained for 90 days
  • Crash reports are retained for 1 year
  • You can delete all data at any time

Children's Privacy

EquiBalance is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes through the app or by email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: info@equibalance.app
  • Website: https://equibalance.app

This Privacy Policy is effective immediately and applies to all users of EquiBalance.
