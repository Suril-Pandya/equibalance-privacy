Privacy Policy for EquiBalance

Last updated: October 18, 2025

Introduction

EquiBalance ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application EquiBalance (the "App").

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Financial Data: Transaction amounts, categories, descriptions, dates, and locations
  • Account Information: Email address, username, and profile information
  • Budget and Goal Data: Financial goals, budget limits, and spending targets

Device Information

  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, feature interactions, and performance data
  • Camera Data: Receipt images captured for expense tracking (processed locally)
  • Audio Data: Voice recordings for expense input (processed locally)

Technical Information

  • Log Data: App crashes, errors, and performance metrics
  • Network Data: IP address, network type, and connection status
  • Analytics Data: App usage statistics and user behavior patterns

How We Use Your Information

Core App Functionality

  • Expense Tracking: Process and categorize your financial transactions
  • Budget Management: Monitor spending against your budget goals
  • Financial Insights: Generate personalized financial reports and recommendations
  • Receipt Scanning: Extract transaction data from receipt images using OCR
  • Voice Input: Convert voice commands to text for expense entry

App Improvement

  • Feature Development: Improve existing features and develop new ones
  • Performance Optimization: Enhance app speed and reliability
  • User Experience: Personalize your app experience
  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with the app

Security and Support

  • Data Security: Protect your information from unauthorized access
  • Customer Support: Provide technical support and respond to inquiries
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal and regulatory requirements

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Primary Storage: All financial data is stored locally on your device
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods
  • Access Control: Data is protected by device security (PIN, fingerprint, face ID)

Cloud Storage (Optional)

  • Backup: Optional cloud backup of your data (with your explicit consent)
  • Sync: Cross-device synchronization (if enabled)
  • Security: Cloud data is encrypted and secured with enterprise-grade security

Data Sharing

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties
  • No Advertising: We do not use your data for advertising purposes
  • No Data Mining: We do not mine your data for commercial purposes

Camera and Microphone Usage

Camera Permission

  • Purpose: Capture receipt images for expense tracking
  • Processing: Images are processed locally using OCR technology
  • Storage: Images are stored locally and can be deleted at any time
  • Sharing: Images are not shared with third parties

Microphone Permission

  • Purpose: Voice input for expense entry
  • Processing: Audio is processed locally for speech-to-text conversion
  • Storage: Audio recordings are not permanently stored
  • Sharing: Audio data is not shared with third parties

Your Rights and Choices

Data Access

  • View Data: Access all your stored financial information
  • Export Data: Download your data in CSV format
  • Data Portability: Transfer your data to other financial apps

Data Control

  • Edit Data: Modify or correct your financial information
  • Delete Data: Remove specific transactions or all data
  • Account Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data

Data Deletion and User Rights

How to Delete Your Data

Method 1: Through the App

  1. Open the EquiBalance app
  2. Go to Settings â†’ Account Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

Method 2: Contact Us Directly

  • Email: privacy@equibalance.app
  • Include your account email address in your request
  • Response time: Within 48 hours

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • All expense transactions and history
  • Budget information and limits
  • Financial goals and targets
  • User profile data (name, email, preferences)
  • App settings and customizations
  • Receipt images and voice recordings
  • All financial insights and reports

Data Retention After Deletion

  • Personal data: Permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized analytics: May be retained for app improvement (no personal identifiers)
  • Legal requirements: Some data may be retained for legal compliance
  • Backup data: Cloud backups deleted within 30 days

Partial Data Deletion

You can also delete specific data without deleting your entire account:

  • Individual expense transactions
  • Specific budget categories
  • Financial goals
  • Receipt images
  • Voice recordings

Data Export Before Deletion

Before deleting your account, you can export all your data:

  1. Go to Settings â†’ Data & Privacy
  2. Tap "Export My Data"
  3. Download your data in CSV format
  4. Save it to your device or cloud storage

Verification of Deletion

After requesting data deletion, you will receive:

  • Confirmation email within 48 hours
  • Final deletion confirmation within 30 days
  • Certificate of data deletion (upon request)

Privacy Settings

  • Camera Access: Enable/disable camera functionality
  • Microphone Access: Enable/disable voice input features
  • Cloud Sync: Control cloud backup and synchronization
  • Analytics: Opt out of usage analytics

Data Retention

Financial Data

  • Retention Period: Data is retained until you delete it
  • Backup Retention: Cloud backups are retained for 30 days after account deletion
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained for legal compliance

Technical Data

  • Logs: App logs are retained for 90 days
  • Analytics: Usage analytics are retained for 2 years
  • Crash Reports: Crash data is retained for 1 year

Children's Privacy

EquiBalance is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

International Users

If you are using EquiBalance from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.
